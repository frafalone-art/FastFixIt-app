# Privacy Policy

**FastFixIt** – Your app for finding local professionals
Last updated: March 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

FastFixIt is a mobile application connecting clients with local professionals for maintenance and repair services. The data controller is **FastFixIt**, reachable at [assistenza@fastfixit.it](mailto:assistenza@fastfixit.it).

---

## 2. Data We Collect

- **Registration data:** first name, last name, username, email address, password (encrypted with bcrypt), phone number, address.
- **Professional data (providers):** business name, owner's name, VAT number, professional category, description, profile photo.
- **Location:** used solely to show nearby professionals. Collected only with your explicit consent.
- **Messages:** in-app chat messages exchanged between clients and providers.
- **Usage data:** service requests, reviews, ratings.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- Provide the app's features (search, bookings, chat).
- Verify your identity via email OTP at registration.
- Send service communications (e.g. password reset, notifications).
- Improve platform quality and security.

---

## 4. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on your explicit consent given at registration and the performance of the usage contract (Art. 6(1)(a) and (b) GDPR).

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

---

## 6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. We use the following technical service providers:

- **Supabase** – database (PostgreSQL), EU servers.
- **Oracle Cloud** – app hosting, Italy (Milan).
- **Resend** – transactional email delivery.

---

## 7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, and delete your data, object to processing, request data portability, and withdraw consent at any time.

---

## 8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures: HTTPS/TLS encryption, bcrypt password hashing, JWT authentication, API rate limiting, and restricted system access.

---

## 9. Minors

FastFixIt is intended for users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 16.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. For significant changes, we will notify you via in-app notification or email.

---
